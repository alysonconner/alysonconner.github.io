Alyson Conner

Designer, Illustrator & Creative

Hi · Hello · Bonjour · Hola

Hi, my name is Alyson Conner. I am a designer, illustrator and creative person living in Ottawa, Canada. I am passionate about my creativity and about design. I love to use my creativity to help others feel confident about what they represent or support. A continuous learning environment keeps me on my toes. I’m driven by the challenges I face as a designer, and by reflecting on a finished product. When I'm not designing I love to play with my kids, or snuggle up and watch a movie at home.
