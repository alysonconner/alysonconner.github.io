Magazine Ad | Iconography · Quebec Science

This was created to appear in Quebec Science magazine. The purpose of the ad is to thank the scientists envolved in the top ten most important discoveries of 2014. This year, those discoveries came from one of nine scientific fields including: cellular biology, the study of grey matter, geology, genetics, physical science, astrology, forrestry, marine biology, and oceanography.

Computer Graphics · Mandala

This Mandala was created as a Computer Graphics Project for school.

A mandala is a spiritual and ritual symbol in Hinduism and Buddhism, representing the Universe. I liked this assignment. I had fun playing with different textures and colours, until I had something I felt was complete. I like the symmetry of the mandala.
